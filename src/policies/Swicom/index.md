# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: Swicom

At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a top priority. This policy explains why and how we collect information about you and how we process such data. Swicom does not collect any personally identifying information.  

#####Who controls your data
ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd and its <span style="color:red"><u>subsidiary companies</span></u> are responsible for your data.

#####The data we collect about you and how we get it 
We do not collect your personal data. 
 
However, Swicom will ask for permission to use your device’s camera. This action needs to be performed in order to allow you to scan QR code which is necessary for the purpose of using the application’s intended functionalities. We will not collect or store any data as result of receiving such permission.  
 
We don’t collect information about your age, gender or any other information that could lead to your identification. We don’t ask our users to log in or authenticate in our app. We also don’t collect information about the device you’re using or it’s geographical location.   
You can just use it without sharing any personal information with us.  

#####What we use your data for 
We do not collect any data about you or your device. It isn’t necessary for us to have such information in order to ensure that the intended functionalities are active.  

#####Data minimization 
Our app doesn’t request any data as it’s not relevant to the core functionality and will only request access to your device’s camera to allow you to scan QR code. We do not request access to protected resources like photos or contacts. As we mentioned, our app can be used without a log-in. We do not store credentials or tokens.  

#####How we share the data  
We do not collect your data and therefore we also have nothing to share. It’s not required for us in order to provide you with the intended Swicom functionalities. 
 
#####How long we keep your data 
As we do not collect your data it will also not be kept by us in any form.  
 
#####Contact and further information 
If you have any questions, you can contact us [www.new.abb.com/contact-centres] (https://new.abb.com/contact-centers)
