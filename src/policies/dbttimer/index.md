
# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: APP for Digital timers - 9ABF0015438 


At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a top priority. This policy explains why and how we collect information about you and how we process such data. Our DBT timer APP for digital timers does not collect any personally identifying information. However, we will gather some information about the location of the device running the app to solely facilitate you to automatically set the appropriate time zone of the ABB timer to configure, but we are not able to connect this information to any particular person, neither retain this information into ABB servers.


### Who controls your data
ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd and its [subsidiary companies](https://new.abb.com/privacy-policy/subsidiaries) are responsible for your data.

###The data we collect about you and how we get it
We do not collect your personal data.
However, ABB DBT Timer app uses information about the location of the device so it would be able to define the time zone to determine daylight saving settings. That will enable you to automatically program the device according to the position detected at the time of using the app. The position detection service is automatically enabled by the DBT timer APP when you are using the app. The position and settings of the timer are just stored on your personal device and not saved on ABB servers.
We don’t collect information about your age, gender or any other information that could lead to your identification. We don’t ask our users to log in or authenticate in our app. You can just use it without sharing any personal information with us.

###What we use your data for
It’s necessary to check the time zone of the device so to allow you to set the appropriate time zone of the ABB timer to configure.

###Data minimization
Our app requests only data relevant to the core functionality and uses data that is required to accomplish the relevant task. We do not request access to protected resources like photos or contacts. As we mentioned, our app can be used without a log-in. We do not store credentials or tokens.

###How we share the data
We only share your data with other ABB affiliates or third parties as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. See the [list of ABB subsidiaries](https://new.abb.com/privacy-policy/subsidiaries)

###How long we keep your data
We only keep your data for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this privacy notice. After this time, we will securely delete the data.

###Information security
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the data. We restrict access to information only to ABB employees and identified parties.

###Contact and further information
If you have any questions, you can contact us [https://new.abb.com/contact-centers](https://new.abb.com/contact-centers)
