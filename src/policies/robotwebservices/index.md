# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: Robot Web Services

At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a top priority. This policy explains why and how we collect information about you and how we process such data. 

We don’t collect any personal data with our Robot Web Services application. By using it you can connect to your Robot.

Our app process only data relevant to the core functionality and uses data that is required to accomplish the relevant task. For this purpose we don’t require you to provide any personally identifying information when using it so we don’t collect or share any information about you or your device. 

###Information security
We always take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the data. We restrict access to information only to ABB employees and identified parties.
