# Privacy Policy
## Health Risk Assessment
###Covid-19

Information about privacy protection

ABB is fully engaged in your privacy protection and respecting your rights to control the way, your personal details are used. In this context we would like to inform you, why the data that you pro-vided with this questionnaire is needed and how is it going to be processed.

ABB SP. z o.o is responsible for your personal details and is controlling the way of its usage, accord-ing to our privacy policy. We process your personal details primarily to maintain/ensure the health and safety of our employees and personnel as well as avoid spreading disease of COVID-19 and the virus SARS-COV-2. The information that we ask you to provide are being used to assess potential risk related to what is mentioned above.

We perform this activities based on our legal responsibility as an Employer, to take care of health protection and safety of our employees. Your personal details are going to be processed upon avoiding transborder health threats or upon ABB’s justified interest. Moreover we can be forced to share your personal data to our Corporate Crisis Team, with a headquarter in Switzerland (excep-tionally some other related entities might be involved,  in a situation where it is necessary for as-sessment or avoidance of potential health risk.

Your questionnaire is going to be instantly removed (latest the end of the day that you visited ABB’s office), if you are going to be asked not to enter our office. If you answered NO to all ques-tions, we can keep your questionnaire for 14 days, to be able to prove that everyone who was let in to ABB’s premise was carefully checked.

To get more information on the way, how we use your personal details and your right to privacy, please visit our full Privacy Policy, under below address:
[https://new.abb.com/privacy-notice/visitor-health-check](https://new.abb.com/privacy-notice/visitor-health-check). 

Thank you for your cooperation to ensure safety in our workspace.
