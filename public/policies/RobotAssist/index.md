# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: Robot Assist


At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a priority. This privacy notice explains why and how we collect information about you and how we process such data.

We don’t collect any personal data with our Cobot application. By using it you can access resources helping you to learn more about ABB products.
Our app processes only data relevant to the core functionality and uses data that is required to accomplish the relevant task. For this purpose we don’t require you to provide any personally identifying information when using it so we don’t collect or share any information about you or your device.


### Information security
We always take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the data. We restrict access to information only to ABB employees and identified parties.


### Contact and further information
If you have any questions or concerns about how ABB processes personal data, you can contact us at <privacy@abb.com> or submit your request at [www.abb.com/privacy](www.abb.com/privacy). You can also contact us via respective Contact Center at our [website](https://new.abb.com/contact-centers).
