# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: FlexArc 3D Catalog



At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a top priority. Our app does not collect any personally identifying information.

###Who controls your data
ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd and its [subsidiary companies](https://new.abb.com/privacy-policy/subsidiaries) are responsible for your data. 

###The data we collect about you and how we get it
We will not collect your personal data. Yet we will ask you for access to your phone in order to identify the device. There is no authentication or authorization – you can use our app without logging in. 

###What we use your data for
We will not use your personal data. We are not going to identify the user but only the device which he/she is using. 

###Data minimization
Our app requests only data relevant to the core functionality and uses data that is required to accomplish the relevant task like access to the camera for device-identification purposes. We do not request access to protected resources like photos or contacts. We do not collect any statistics.

###How we share the data 
We only share data with other ABB affiliates or third parties as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. See the  [list of ABB subsidiaries](https://new.abb.com/privacy-policy/subsidiaries).

###How long we keep your data
No personal data is being stored. What is more we do not store any statistics or other information that could lead us to direct identification of the user.

###Information security
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the data. We restrict access to information only to ABB employees and identified parties.

###Contact and further information
If you have any questions, you can contact us [https://new.abb.com/contact-centers](https://new.abb.com/contact-centers)
