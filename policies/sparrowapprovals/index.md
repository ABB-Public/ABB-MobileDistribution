# Privacy Policy
## Applicability: Sparrow Approvals

At ABB, respecting your data privacy rights is a top priority. This policy explains why and how we collect information about you and how we process such data. Drivetune does not collect any personally identifying information. However, we will gather information about the country from which you are using our app, the device you are using and how you use our app. 

###Who controls your data 
ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd and its subsidiary companies are responsible for your data. 

###The data we collect about you and how we get it 
We will not collect your personal data. 

But still we have to get some information about our users for statistical reasons. Because of that we may collect limited non-personally identifying information that your device makes available whenever you use our app. 

That is why we will collect information about the location of your device but only regarding the country from which you are using our app. This will be gathered automatically, when you are using the app. 

What's more? We also can get some information about the device you are using - brand of the device, category (is it a mobile or a tablet?), model, OS version and language used by the device. 

And finally, we may collect data about how the app is used - from which app store it was downloaded, what is the version of the app, when you opened it for the first time and what functionalities are you using. 

We don't collect information about your age, gender or any other information that could lead to your identification. We don't ask our users to log in or authenticate in our app. You can just use it without sharing any personal information with us. 

###What we use your data for 
We use information about a country from which you are using our app to get a minimal knowledge about our users. It's just for statistics yet in future this may help us to provide better and better service for you. 

We use information about the device and how the app is used to improve our performance and service. 

###Data minimization 
Our app requests only data relevant to the core functionality and uses data that is required to accomplish the relevant task. We do not request access to protected resources like photos or contacts. As we mentioned, our app can be used without a log-in. We do not store credentials or tokens. 

###How we share the data 
We only share your data with other ABB affiliates or third parties as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. See the list of ABB subsidiaries 

###How long we keep your data 
We only keep your data for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this privacy notice. After this time, we will securely delete the data. 

###Information security 
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access or against unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of the data. We restrict access to information only to ABB employees and identified parties. 

###Contact and further information 
If you have any questions, you can contact us [https://new.abb.com/contact-centers](http://www.abb.com/privacy)
